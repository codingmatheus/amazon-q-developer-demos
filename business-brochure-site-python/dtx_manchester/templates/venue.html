Venue Information

DTX Manchester will be held at Manchester Central, one of the UK's leading conference and exhibition venues. Located in the heart of Manchester, this iconic venue offers world-class facilities in a historic setting.

Manchester Central

Manchester Central is housed in what was formerly Manchester Central railway station, a historic landmark in the city. The venue combines Victorian architecture with modern facilities, creating a unique setting for events.

Getting There

By Train

Manchester Central is a 5-minute walk from Oxford Road train station, a 10-minute walk from Deansgate train station, and a 20-minute walk from Manchester Piccadilly train station.

By Tram

The nearest Metrolink tram stops are Deansgate-Castlefield and St Peter's Square, both of which are a 5-minute walk from the venue.

By Car

Manchester Central is easily accessible from major motorways. The venue is a 20-minute drive from the M60 orbital motorway, and parking is available at the NCP Manchester Central car park adjacent to the venue.

By Air

Manchester Airport is approximately 9 miles (14.5 km) from Manchester Central. A taxi journey takes around 25 minutes, while the train from the airport to Oxford Road station takes approximately 20 minutes.
